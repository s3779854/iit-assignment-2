In dept with Data Analyst

In this part, we will focus on one occupation which is Data Analyst. According to Bridging the gap article there is a similarity with Data Analyst and System Analyst. System Analyst is top 5 with 1,929 job post from August 2018 to August 2019. Both occupation roles required then to analyse business processes and providing support to remote users, monitors the internal system daily ensuring it accuracy and availability, and required to do regular reporting and document management, both occupation needs understanding, designing and coding for different client based tools used on their system. A fact that Business Analyst and System Analyst needs to work together with the IT Team to produce a solution that are consistent and variable to current issues. In terms of skills and knowledge, they are required to have knowledge and experience with SQL (Structure Query Language), Oracle, and programming however with System Analyst, it requires more broad technical knowledge as they work most closely with technology and required to maintain the database of the company while data analyst is more on interpreting a report from the database.

In the future if Data Analyst want to pursue another career within the jobs that are on demands according to the Burning Glass data, they can pursue to Data Scientist after having an experience in coding. Data Scientist is on top 8 with 1,339 job post. It has been seen that Data Analyst is a stepping stone to become a Data Scientist as Data Analyst person is seen great in numbers however need to sharpen their skills in data modelling and coding skills. Becoming a Data scientist must excel in these field.
